ABOUT JARVIS

THE ORIGIN

J.A.R.V.I.S — inspired by Tony Stark's iconic AI companion from the Marvel universe — is a Python-based voice assistant built to bring that futuristic feeling to your desktop.

Wake it up with a voice command, and it's ready to execute your every digital command — from web searches and app launches to reading the news and checking the weather.

Built with passion as a Python learning project, JARVIS combines multiple powerful libraries into one seamless voice-driven experience.

" Good Morning sir, please tell me how can I help you? "
